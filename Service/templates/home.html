Querying Details:

Querying Details of a Program by ProgramID:
Route: /program_details/<int:program_id>
Example URL: http://localhost:5000/program_details/1 (Replace 1 with the desired ProgramID)

Querying Evaluation Results by Semester:
Route: /evaluation_results/<int:program_id>/<semester>
Example URL: http://localhost:5000/evaluation_results/1/Fall (Replace 1 and Fall with the desired ProgramID and Semester)

Querying Departments:
Route: /query_departments
Example URL: http://localhost:5000/query_departments

Querying Evaluation Results by Academic Year:
Route: /evaluation_results/<academic_year>
Example URL: http://localhost:5000/evaluation_results/23-24 (Replace 23-24 with the desired academic year)
